My Story

Eggs. Something about them just make my heart sing. I love cracking eggs cooking eggs and most of all eating eggs. One day, Grandma Pauley had a vision of Kira standing in front of a stove cooking eggs. And that was it. I knew what my destiny was. Eggs. and eggs only.

Kira Pauley Owner and Executive Chef
